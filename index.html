About Cupcake Boss

Cupcake Boss is a family owned bakery established by the Valastro family in 1991. Because of the Valastro family’s dedication to quality and excellence, Cupcake Boss has received national recognition. Cupcake Boss makes the best darn cupcakes in the world, bar none–it’s just a fact. You can even customize your own cupcake with a variety of flavors and toppings to choose from. So, what are you waiting for? Order now!
